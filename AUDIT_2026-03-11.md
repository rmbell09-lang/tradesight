## Algorithm Logic Concern

Top-scoring signals are near-50/50 markets with high volume:
- "Will bitcoin hit $1M before GTA VI?" — 0.4885/0.5115, conf 0.993
- "Will Jesus Christ return before GTA VI?" — 0.485/0.515, conf 0.991
- "Russia x Ukraine ceasefire by end of 2026?" — 0.365/0.635, conf 0.919

**The algorithm is scoring confidence based on volume/liquidity, not price edge.** A high-volume near-50/50 market has no clear directional edge — it's not a "high opportunity" just because it's popular.

**Real opportunity = price mispricing + edge** (e.g., market says 35% but you think 60%). Pure volume correlation produces false signals.

## Recommendations

1. **Fix `expected_profit` calculation** — need entry price, exit price, position size
2. **Fix `signal_price_yes/no` capture** — store prices at signal time, not NULL
3. **Fix duplicate insertion bug** — add UNIQUE constraint on `(market_id, signal_time)`
4. **Redesign confidence scoring** — base on price deviation from "fair value" estimate, not raw volume
5. **Add paper trade tracking table** — `paper_trades(market_id, side, entry_price, exit_price, size, pnl)`
6. **Backfill with more data** — single day is not enough for pattern analysis

---

## Verdict

Paper trading infrastructure exists but is not production-ready. Core P&L tracking is broken (prices not stored, profit always 0). Algorithm needs a fundamental logic change before results are meaningful. Good bones — needs ~1 week of fixes before it can generate usable signals.
